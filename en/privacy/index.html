Privacy Policy

What is our privacy policy?
We take our users' privacy extremely seriously and do not share or sell your personal information to third parties or unrelated companies. We are committed to respecting your right to privacy. Information to third parties may be provided only if it is required by the competent authorities in accordance with the procedures laid down in the legislation of the Republic of Bulgaria.
What information do we collect from visitors?
For each visitor, we collect statistical information including device type, operating system, version and model of browser used, time spent on pages, location, and more. For some of the actions performed through the functionality of the system, the visitor's IP address is also preserved. Demographic information about visitors by type of gender, age, professed religion, interests, behaviors, etc. is not stored.
For what purposes do we use the information?
The information we collect is used to improve the services provided, statistical reports and feedback.
Cookies
We use cookies to analyze user behavior and improve the functionality offered. Cookies contain a small amount of information stored in the registers of your web browser and in no way can damage your system or install harmful software. Cookies can only be requested and accessed from the website from which they are issued.
Who do we share your personal information with?
We do not share or sell your personal information to third parties or unrelated companies. However, the website operates using certain services of other companies. Your personal information is shared when you give your permission.
