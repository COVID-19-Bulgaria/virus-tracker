Privacy Policy

What is our privacy policy?
We take our users' privacy extremely seriously and do not share or sell your personal information to third parties or unrelated companies. We are committed to respecting your right to privacy. Information to third parties may be provided only if it is required by the competent authorities in accordance with the procedures laid down in the legislation of the Republic of Bulgaria.
What information do we collect from visitors?
For each visitor, we collect statistical information including device type, operating system, version and model of browser used, time spent on pages, location, anonymized (incomplete) IP address and more. This information is anonymized and cannot be correlated to a specific visitor. Cookies are not used and demographic information about visitors by type of gender, age, professed religion, interests, behaviors, etc. is not stored.
For what purposes do we use the information?
The information we collect is used to improve the services provided, statistical reports and feedback.
Change in privacy policy
We reserve the right to make any changes to our privacy policy. Changes will be announced on the current page. The user should proactively check the current privacy policy for updates.
